Planetegem Painter

Welcome to Planetegem Painter, an online paint simulator made in javascript. No external libraries were used: everything was custom-built and written entirely from the ground up.

Once started, you can switch between a selection tool and a draw tool. The selection tool gives you the ability to import images and delete them. You can also move the images, rotate them or change their scale. The images were taken from Heritage Type. The draw tool will allow you to paint on the canvas. You can switch between drawing on the foreground and background, and can pick a color with the colorwheel & a size and shape for your brush using the sliders.

Click continue to close this screen and start painting. If you need to read these instructions again, you can click the 'i' in the upper left corner.
